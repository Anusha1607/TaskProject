Cafe GoodLuck is a popular Irani cafe-restaurant on the Fergusson College Road, in the Deccan Gymkhana area of Pune, India.Established in 1935 by Hussain Ali Yakshi, it is one of the oldest Irani places in Pune.Cafe GoodLuck is most famous for it bun maska/bun omelette and Iranian tea among the other dishes.The place is always crowded and especially popular among the youngsters.Cafe GoodLuck has no other branch.
